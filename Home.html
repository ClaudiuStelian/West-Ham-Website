West Ham United University
Our aim is to offer Education and Research solutions at a high quality standard, in an intellectually stimulating internal climate for both students and faculty.
If you want to become a student in United Kingdom, immerse into the British culture and finally learn English language, it means you are ready to embark into the experience of your life. You can do that at the West HHam United University in London, where you have the chance to enroll for a one year English language program. The university also offers courses of English language, focusing both on specialized vocabulary (CertHE in Skills for the workplace and Health and Social Care,) and grammar, that will offer students the possibility for a visible language improvement.
